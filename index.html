How it works

One press. One habit.
One honest count.

📝

Name your habits

Write your daily tasks on the task-row slips. Change them whenever you like — no app update, no settings menu. Just write.

🔘

Press when done

Each row has a tactile button. Press it when you've completed that habit. The LED lights up. Press again to undo. It counts from the button, not the screen.

✅

Win your day

Complete all your habits and the screen reads "Today's day WON." Miss any by 8pm and it's honest with you. Reset at midnight, automatically.

No distractions

A device that only does one thing

HabitBoard doesn't notify you, doesn't have a feed, doesn't have a settings page. It sits on your desk and waits for you to press a button. That's it.

Tactile feedback

You feel the progress

There's something different about pressing a physical button versus tapping a screen. Each click is a small, satisfying commitment. The LED turning on makes it real.

Fully customisable

Your habits, your labels

Slide in handwritten or printed slips behind each task row. Change your habits monthly, weekly, or daily — no login required, no sync, no friction.

Always-on display

LCD screen shows you where you stand

A 1602 LCD at the top of the device shows your current progress count and status message at all times. No wake button, no lock screen.
